Saving Snow White

Thw Evil Queen is throwing the poisoned apples.
Your goal is to save Snow White by avoiding the poisoned apples.
If you manage to save the Snow White, she will meet the Prince!

How To Play?

  1. Start the game by pressing the START button
  2. Press the LEFT and RIGHT arrow key to avoid the apples
  3. You only have 3 chance
  4. You can STOP the game anytime by pressing STOP button
  5. Let's PLAY!

Congratulations!
